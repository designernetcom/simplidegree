Benefits of the online M.Sc

If you do not want to halt your career to accomplish your academic goals, online M.Sc is an ideal programme for you. To make the decision easy for you, we have compiled few benefits of pursuing online M.Sc at this point of your career:

  • Flexibility: The most important reason why people go for online education. The course plan includes live lectures, multimedia content, digital libraries and gives access to multiple learning resources. You can learn from anywhere by simply making a little bit of adjustment in your routines and without compromising on your responsibilities.
  • Global Networking Opportunity: In online programmes, webinars and guest lectures are often conducted by academicians and industry experts from across the globe, which gives you the opportunity to build new contacts and widen your network.
  • Cost-Effective: Online M.Sc programme has a lower tuition fee compared to traditional programmes plus there is no travelling expense and relocation cost.
  • Individualised Learning: Here you have full control of your learning experience, so you can plan your course according to your personal goals. In fact, it gives you enough liberty to go beyond the classroom lessons and expand your knowledge base.

Eligibility criteria and entrance exams

You may find different admission process and eligibility criteria in different colleges. Therefore, we have provided a general criteria followed by most of the institutions:

A bachelor's degree in a relevant field is usually required. For example, if you're applying for an MSc in Computer Science, a bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field is generally preferred but not compulsory.

Many universities specify a GPA or minimum marks of 50% to apply for the programme.

Admission process

Admission is made on a rolling basis with a fixed deadline for applying. The admission calendar and structure is different for each university. Nonetheless, below mentioned is a general overview of the admissions process followed largely:

Both government and private institutions offer the online M.Sc programme, so the fee range varies significantly between ₹ 25,000 to ₹ 1,00,000 for the full course. Apart from the academic fee, few institutions have examination fee as well.

Importance of Choosing the Right M.Sc Specialisation

Picking the right online MSc specialisation is like charting a course on a high seas adventure. A well-aligned specialisation deepens your expertise, making you a sought-after professional in your chosen field. Below are a few reasons why choosing a specialisation is a must:

  • Higher Earning Potential - Specialising in a particular field opens doors to niche roles that tend to offer higher salary packages, making you more attractive to employers.
  • Job-Focused Curriculum - An online M.Sc. program with a specific specialisation equips you with the skills and knowledge tailored for the workplace, ensuring you are job-ready.
  • Enhanced Career Prospects - Employers value candidates with in-depth subject knowledge and niche skills, and pursuing a specialised course helps you stand out, unlocking better career opportunities.
  • Increased Motivation - Studying a subject that interests you keeps you engaged and motivated, reducing distractions and inspiring continuous learning.

Subjects and Syllabus

The M.Sc programmes in India cover a diverse range of fields based on which the syllabus of each course differs. Frequently Asked Question

What is an online MSc?

An online Master of Science (M.Sc) is a postgraduate academic degree that can be earned through distance or online learning. It mirrors traditional M.Sc programs but allows students to study remotely via online platforms.

Can MSc be done in correspondence?

Yes, MSc programs can be offered through correspondence or distance education mode also.

What are the most popular Online M.Sc Programs?

Popular online M.Sc programs cover topics including Computer Science, Data Science, Psychology, Finance, Artificial Intelligence, and Environmental Science among others.

What is the course duration?

The duration of online MSc programs is generally 2 years, but for some programs offering part-time options to accommodate working professionals, the duration can increase.
