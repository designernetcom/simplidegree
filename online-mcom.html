Benefits of the online M. Com

Amidst the changing economic atmosphere and business environment, commerce has become a lucrative field with a dire need for competent professionals to adopt the new trends in the market. Therefore, pursuing online Master of Commerce (M.Com) programme at this juncture will benefit you in many ways:

  • Career Advancement: M.Com is a highly sought-after profession required in almost every sector. The programme equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary for leadership roles and ensures a higher earning potential.
  • Enhanced Knowledge and Skills: What you may have learned in your bachelor's programme was just the overview, but the curriculum of the M.Com programme is vast and covers the practical implications of every subject. This will equip you with skills and knowledge required by recruiters, thus making you job-ready.
  • Increased Career Flexibility: As commerce is a vast field, several career prospects open up for you post-completion of this degree. With a master's degree, you can negotiate for a higher salary package and a high-rank position compared to your previous designation.
  • Earn While you Learn: With the flexibility of online learning, you can continue to experience the nitty-gritty of the industry while expanding your academic credentials. This is a great way to practically understand the concepts learned in class and apply them in real-time at work.
  • Affordability: If you have a tight budget or simply don't want to spend much on your master's degree, then online M.Com is the right fit for you. Compared to regular in-campus programmes, online courses have a lower fee structure and you won't have any travel or relocation expenses.

Eligibility criteria and entrance exams

The candidates who wish to pursue online M.Com programme, should thoroughly check the eligibility criteria for every college/university. The general eligibility criteria for this programme is:

A candidate who has completed 10+2 followed by 3-year bachelor's degree programme is eligible to apply.

He/she must have passed his last education with a minimum of 50% marks. The minimum marks criteria may vary for reserved category candidates.

Although it is not mandatory for a candidate to have bachelor's degree in the same field but, he/she should have the basic knowledge of the field and its related concepts.

Importance of Choosing the Right M.Com Specialisation

Choosing the right specialisation for your online Master of Commerce (M.Com) degree is crucial for your future career success and personal satisfaction. Here are some reasons why it matters:

  • Career Pathway: Specialising in a particular field makes you a subject matter expert in that area, making you more competitive in the job market. Employers often seek individuals with niche skills, giving you an edge over others.
  • Higher Salary Package: A candidate with niche skills and a high degree of knowledge base is always preferred by employers. This will land you better opportunities and a lucrative salary package.
  • Better Career Planning: Choosing a specialisation early helps you map out your career path and focus your job search efforts on relevant opportunities. Participating in relevant internships, attending industry events, and networking with professionals within your chosen specialisation can help you build a strong career network and gain valuable experience.
  • Gain High Credibility: Having a degree in a particular area reflects your competency and potential to perform better than those with a general idea of the concepts. It is an added advantage for you when applying for a job.

Career opportunities

Online M.Com paves your way for several job opportunities across various sectors of banking and financial services. Since finance and accounting professionals are required in every company, you have multiple job roles to apply for. Here are a few avenues you can explore:

Job Roles Job Description
Cost Accountant A professional who is involved in calculating the cost associated with a particular service or any activity associated with it. They have to evaluate the overall profit and loss statement while preparing a budget.
Operations Research Analyst Gathers financial data on a business's main operations, evaluates it to identify and address operational costs, and develops and implements solutions to improve efficiency, productivity, and cost-effectiveness.
Risk Manager Identifies, analyzes, and mitigates risks that threaten the organization's financial health, develops and implements risk management strategies to minimize impact and protect assets and reputation.
Corporate Controller Manages the organization's accounting and financial operations, prepares financial statements, manages budgets and forecasts, and ensures compliance with all applicable financial regulations.
Investment Banker Helps individuals, organizations, government or other business entities to increase their capital by providing investment strategies and plans. They act as a bridge between the two parties.
Corporate Secretary A skilled professional who has to monitor every aspect of the company's actions with respect to government rules and regulations. His day-to-day duties include filing tax returns, keeping records, and advising the higher management on the financial decisions of the company.
Internal Auditor In this job role, the candidate has to oversee the internal affairs of the company. They have to evaluate sales, manufacturing, budget, growth, performance of the employee and other things.

Frequently Asked Question

What is an online M.Com?

An online M.Com is a postgraduate-level program suitable for students who wish to make a career in financial services, banking, accounting, and the insurance sector. The program offers flexibility and accessibility, making it ideal for working professionals or students with other commitments.

Which university is best for M.Com correspondence?

Correspondence mode is generally referred to as the distance mode of education, and institutions like Delhi University, Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Amity University, and Manipal Academy of Higher Education, among many other colleges, offer this course in distance mode.

Is online M.Com valid in India?

Yes, online M.Com degrees are valid in India as long as the program is accredited by the UGC-DEB and AIU. The validity of the degree is not affected by the mode of delivery (online or on-campus).

How much does an online M.Com cost?

The cost of an online M.Com program differs in every college, but generally, the total course fee is between ₹6,000 to ₹1,50,000.
