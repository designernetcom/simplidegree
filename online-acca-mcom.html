Benefits of an Online M.Com (with ACCA)

The benefits of this programme underscore the nature of the course, highlighting its global recognition to equip students with a competitive edge in the field of finance and accounting.

  • The ACCA Advantage: Pursuing a master's degree with ACCA accreditation grants exemptions from 9 papers in the knowledge or skill level exams. Additionally, it provides access to a global network of professionals through international communities. This program emphasizes professional values, ethics, and governance, setting it apart from other courses.
  • Global Opportunities: With ACCA's strong international presence, an online M.Com (with ACCA) offers global exposure. This allows you to apply for ACCA-certified accounting or finance roles worldwide, provided you meet the qualifications.
  • Flexibility of Online Learning: Online learning provides the flexibility and self-paced structure needed for a complex and vast program like ACCA. This is especially beneficial for candidates preparing for additional ACCA papers alongside their studies.
  • Better Growth Opportunities: An ACCA-accredited degree enhances your professional credibility, making you a sought-after accountant. This credibility allows you to negotiate competitive salary packages and be considered for top leadership positions in the industry.

Eligibility criteria and entrance exams

There are some criteria to be followed in order to pursue online M. Com with ACCA which is as follow:

  • Educational Qualification: Students must have completed bachelor's degree or other equivalent degree of three-year in any stream from a recognised board. His/her minimum marks should be over 50% (45% for reserved categories). Though there may be few colleges who prefer bachelor's degree in commerce field.
  • Students in their final semester can also apply for this online programme.

Frequently Asked Question

Can I do M.Com and ACCA together?

Yes, you can pursue M.Com and ACCA simultaneously. Pursuing M.Com with ACCA offers advantages as the curriculum is aligned with ACCA, making you eligible for exemptions in many papers and easing the process of doing both.

Can commerce students do ACCA?

Yes, anyone from a commerce background can pursue ACCA. Students must have completed 12th with commerce education from a recognized board or institution to be eligible for ACCA.

What are the benefits of pursuing M.Com with ACCA?

Pursuing M.Com with ACCA gives you an edge in the finance and accounting industry. It enhances your knowledge of accounting principles, taxation, finance management, and ethics. Additionally, the ACCA qualification adds global credibility to your degree, making you competitive in the international job market.

How much time does it take to complete ACCA along with M.Com?

It typically takes 2 years to complete M.Com. As for ACCA, it usually takes 3 to 4 years, depending on the number of exams you clear per year. If pursued simultaneously, the time commitment can overlap, leading to a flexible timeline based on individual progress.
